Help Center

Find answers to common questions and learn how to get the most out of Ripple.

How do I create an account?

Click on "Sign Up" in the navigation bar and fill out the registration form. You'll need to provide your email address, username, and create a password.

How do I complete my profile?

After signing up, go to your profile page and add your skills, upload evidence, and write a bio. A complete profile helps others find and connect with you.

How do I find skills to learn?

Use the "Discover Skills" feature to swipe through available skills, or browse classes in the "Browse Classes" section.

How do I become a teacher?

Go to "Apply to Teach" in the footer or navigation menu. Fill out the application form with your teaching experience and qualifications. Our team will review your application.

How do I create a class?

Once approved as a teacher, you can create classes from your dashboard. Click "Create Class" and fill out the class details including title, description, price, and schedule.

How do I get paid?

Payment processing details will be provided after your teacher application is approved. We handle all transactions securely.

How do I join a community?

Browse communities on the Communities page and click "Join" on any community you're interested in. You can also request to create a new community.

How do I create a community?

Click "Request Community" and fill out the form with your community idea. Our team will review and approve communities that align with our guidelines.

What can I do in a community?

You can post questions, share resources, comment on posts, and connect with other members who share your interests.

How do I change my password?

Go to your profile settings and click "Change Password". You'll need to enter your current password and your new password.

How do I update my profile picture?

Go to your profile page and click "Edit Profile". You can upload a new profile picture from there.

How do I delete my account?

Contact our support team at support@ripple.com to request account deletion. We'll process your request within 7 business days.

I can't log in to my account

Make sure you're using the correct email and password. If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.

I'm not receiving email notifications

Check your spam folder first. If you still don't see emails, verify your email address in your account settings and ensure notifications are enabled.

A feature isn't working

Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. If the problem persists, contact our support team.
